B — Soft Structural

Vercel/Arc Browser aesthetic. Warm white background, floating white cards with subtle shadows, generous border-radius (14-16px). Feels modern and approachable. Active nav uses solid fill. Soft color badges for status.

C — Hybrid Contrast

Dark sidebar + light content area (Stripe/GitHub style). Asymmetric bento grid for metrics. Warm off-white background (#F5F5F0). Combines the authority of dark chrome with the clarity of a light workspace. Best of both worlds.
